Before Vaccination

Make sure to register for the vaccine and book a slot on CO-WIN portal else it may take long.
Wear approximate clothes(Short-sleeved shirts) for easier vaccination.
Hydrate Yourself sufficiently And Have A proper meal.
Consult a medical professional before receiving the shot if you are pregnant,have a congenital disease or allergic to drug.
Carry your Aadhar and 4 digit secret code generated at time of registration on cowin portal.
Avoid all beverage that contains caffeine and alcohol.
Do not take the vaccine if you are suffering from COVID.
